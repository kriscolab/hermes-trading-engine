## The Premise

Crypto is following the same institutionalization path gold did in 2004-2011 after GLD launched. BTC and ETH spot ETFs are the GLD moment. Institutional flows are sticky, structural, and compound over years.

### Why This Works

1. **Named authority.** Larry Fink called BTC "digitizing gold" and launched IBIT — the most successful ETF launch in history.
2. **Falsifiable.** Either institutional flows continue (thesis intact) or reverse (thesis broken). Measurable via ETF flow data.
3. **Precedent.** Gold post-GLD: ETF → institutional adoption → structural appreciation. Same playbook.
4. **Data-backed.** All triggers from free sources: yfinance, CoinGecko, FRED, alternative.me.

### What Invalidates

- ETF outflows > 0.03% BTC mcap for 2 weeks → reduce core 50%
- Major institution exits crypto or BTC < 200-week SMA → exit all
- BTC/SPY correlation > 0.8 for 30 days → thesis pause
- Fink/BlackRock materially reverses crypto stance → re-evaluate
