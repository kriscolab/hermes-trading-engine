## Thesis Summary

**Claim:** AGI is a 2027 event; superintelligence is a 2028–2030 event. Markets have not priced in the exponential trendlines or the industrial mobilisation they require. The primary investment opportunity is NOT in AI model developers or applications — it's in the physical infrastructure bottlenecks.

**Regime:** Exponential compute scaling. Three compounding vectors: raw compute, algorithmic efficiency, and "unhobbling" (removing constraints like context windows). Combined, these compress AGI timelines far ahead of market consensus.

**The bet:** Buy infrastructure bottlenecks (power, compute, memory, optics, fabs). Short what AI eats (legacy tech, commoditized software, industries AI automates away).

**Key differentiator from commodity thesis:** Commodity thesis bets on MOLECULES (energy, metals, oil). AI thesis bets on ELECTRONS + SILICON (compute, chips, optics). Together they cover both sides of the AI build-out.
